# Workload Dashboard

The dashboard gives owners and admins a real-time view of agency revenue and team workload. Planners see a different version focused on their own clients and upcoming tasks.

---

## Admin view

The admin dashboard has two main sections.

### Revenue summary

At the top of the page:

- **On the Books** -- total contract value of all Booked and Active Planning clients
- **Collected** -- total payments marked paid across all active clients
- **Balance Remaining** -- difference between on the books and collected
- **Upcoming payments** -- payments due in the next 30 days, listed by client and amount

This is actual contracted revenue, not pipeline projections. Leads and Proposal Sent stages are not included.

### Pipeline forecast

Below the summary, the pipeline section shows how many leads are at each stage and the estimated value at each point. Use this to gauge how full the coming months look.

---

## Planner view

The planner execution dashboard is focused on active weddings:

- **Needs Attention** -- clients with gaps that need action: missing vendors, stalling checklists, no upcoming meetings scheduled, or a proposal sitting unsigned too long
- **Wedding Execution Status** -- a table of all active clients sorted by wedding date, with checklist progress, vendor status, and payment status at a glance

Clicking any client row opens their full profile.

---

## Switching views

Owners and admins see the revenue view by default. There's a button at the top of the dashboard to switch to the planner execution view, so you can see what your planners see without switching accounts. Switch back with the button that appears in the planner view.

---

## Team workload

The admin view includes a workload summary showing how many active clients are assigned to each planner. Use this when deciding who to assign a new client to and to spot if anyone's caseload is getting unmanageable.
