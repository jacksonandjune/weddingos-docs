# Notes & Updates

The activity log on every client profile keeps a running record of what's happened on that account. It's how you and your team stay in sync without having to ask each other what's going on.

---

## What gets logged automatically

WeddingOS logs certain events without any action from you:

- Status changes (Lead to Consultation Scheduled, Booked, etc.)
- Proposal sent and signed
- Planner assigned or reassigned
- Vendor updates (added, booked, status changed)
- Payment marked paid
- 30-day and 7-day wedding approach reminders sent

---

## Adding a manual note

Open a client's profile and scroll to the activity section, or look for the **Add Note** option. Type your note and save. Notes are visible to all team members with access to that client.

Use notes for anything that happened outside the system -- a phone call, a venue walkthrough, a conversation with a vendor, a decision the couple made verbally. If it's relevant to the wedding, it belongs in the log.

---

## Who sees the activity log

Anyone with access to the client record can see the full activity log. For planners, that means their assigned clients. Owners and admins see all client logs.

---

## Tips

- Log notes right after calls and meetings while details are fresh.
- Check the log before reaching out to a client. It quickly shows when you last talked and what was discussed.
- If you're handing a client to a different planner, a thorough log makes the transition much smoother.
